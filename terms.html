Scope of Inspection

Our inspections are visual, non-invasive examinations of the accessible areas of a residential property. We inspect readily accessible components of the following systems:

  • Structure and foundation (visible areas)
  • Roof, attic, and insulation
  • Electrical systems
  • Plumbing systems
  • Heating, ventilation, and cooling (HVAC)
  • Exterior and interior components
  • Windows, doors, and finishes

We do not perform destructive testing, move furniture or personal belongings, inspect concealed or inaccessible areas, or provide engineering analysis.

Standards of Practice

All inspections are conducted in accordance with the standards of practice set out by the Home Inspectors Association of British Columbia (HIABC).

Report Delivery

Your written inspection report will be delivered electronically within 24 hours of the completed inspection. The report is based on observable conditions at the time of inspection and should not be interpreted as a guarantee of any component's future performance.
