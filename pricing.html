Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

How do I know the size of my home? +
You can find the square footage on the MLS listing, your property assessment, or the strata/building documents. If you're not sure, give us a call and we'll help you figure it out.
Is the thermal imaging really included? +
Yes — 100%. We use a FLIR thermal camera on every inspection at no extra charge. It helps us detect hidden moisture, insulation gaps, and electrical hotspots that a visual inspection alone can miss.
When do I pay? +
Payment is collected when you book online through our booking system. We accept major credit cards. Your booking is confirmed once payment is processed.
How long does an inspection take? +
Most inspections take 2–3.5 hours depending on home size and complexity. We never rush. You're welcome to attend and ask questions throughout.
When do I receive my report? +
Your full digital report is typically delivered within 24 hours of the inspection. It includes photos, findings, and clear explanations of everything we found.
What's your cancellation policy? +
We require at least 48 hours notice for cancellations or reschedules. See our full cancellation policy for details.
