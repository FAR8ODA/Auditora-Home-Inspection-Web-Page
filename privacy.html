Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Who We Are

Auditora Home Inspection ("we," "our," or "us") is a home inspection company based in Greater Vancouver, BC, Canada. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website (auditora.ca) or our services.

What Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you book an inspection, fill out our contact form, or communicate with us:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • The property address for your inspection
  • Payment information (processed securely through Square — we do not store card details)
  • Any messages or notes you send us

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Schedule and confirm your home inspection
  • Send you your inspection report and follow-up communication
  • Respond to your questions or requests
  • Process your payment
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations in British Columbia

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not send marketing emails without your consent.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information. Payments are handled through Square, which uses industry-standard encryption. We limit access to your information to only what is necessary to provide our services.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may collect data as part of their normal operation:

  • Square — for booking and payment processing
  • Google Analytics (if enabled) — for website traffic insights

Each of these services has its own Privacy Policy governing how they handle data.

Your Rights (BC PIPA)

Under British Columbia's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for certain uses of your information

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@auditora.ca.
