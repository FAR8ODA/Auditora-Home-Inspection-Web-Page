My Story

Hi, I'm Vahid — Founder of Auditora

I started Auditora Home Inspection with one simple goal: to give people in Greater Vancouver a home inspector they can actually trust. Someone who shows up prepared, explains things clearly, and puts your interests first — not a quick turnaround.

I trained through HIABC and Carson Dunlop, two of the most respected names in the inspection industry. I've invested in professional-grade tools including a FLIR thermal imaging camera and moisture meters because I believe a real inspection goes beyond what the eye can see.

"A home is one of the biggest investments of your life. You deserve honest, detailed information — not a rushed walkthrough."

Every inspection I do, I approach the same way: methodically, thoroughly, and with clear communication before, during, and after. You're welcome to attend your inspection and ask me anything along the way.

I serve clients across Vancouver, Burnaby, North Vancouver, Richmond, Coquitlam, and the entire Lower Mainland. I built Auditora to be the kind of company I'd want to hire myself.
